EBA SIG

A Special Interest Group for the Association of Behavior Analysis International

  • Ethics of Applied Behavior Analysis

    Enhance the current state of ethical conduct for all individuals engaged in Applied Behavior Analysis.

  • Ethical Decision Making

    Facilitation of a behavior analytic approach to ethical decision making.

  • Behavior Analytic Approach

    Promote an experimental behavior analytic and empirical approach to understanding ethical behavior.

RECENT ETHICS & BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS PUBLICATIONS

  • Culture

    Hughes Fong, E., Catagnus, R.M., Brodhead, M.T., Quigley, S., & Field, S. (2016). Developing the Cultural Awareness Skills of Behavior Analysts. Behavior Analysis in Practice, 9, 84-94.

    de Melo, C.M., Lopes Bezerra de Castro, M.S., & de Rose, J.C. (2015). Some Relations Between Culture, Ethics, and Technology in B.F. Skinner. Behavior and Social Issues, 24, 39-55.

  • Interdisciplinary Settings

    Brodhead, M.T. (2015). Maintaining Professional Relationships in an Interdisciplinary Setting: Strategies for Navigating Nonbehavioral Treatment Recommendations for Individuals with Autism. Behavior Analysis in Practice, 8, 70-78.

  • Punishment

    Apel, A.B., & Diller, J.W. (2016). Prison as punishment: A behavior-analytic evaluation of incarceration. The Behavior Analyst. doi: 10.1007/s40614-016-0081-6

    Critchfield, T.S. (2014). Skeptic's Corner: Punishment - Destructive Force or Valuable Social "Adhesive"? Behavior Analysis in Practice, 7, 36-44.

  • Social Media

    O'Leary et al. (2015). Blurred Lines: Ethical Implications of Social Media for Behavior Analysis. Behavior Analysis in Practice

  • Supervision

    Turner, L.B., Fischer, A.J., & Luiselli, J.JK. (2016). Towards a Competency-Based, Ethical, and Socially Valid Approach to the Supervision of Applied Behavior Analytic Trainees. Behavior Analysis in Practice, 9, 287-298.

    Sellers, T.P., Alai-Rosales, S. & MacDonald, R.P.F. (2016). Taking Full Responsibility: the Ethics of Supervision in Behavior Analytic Practice. Behavior Analysis in Practice, 9, 299-308.
